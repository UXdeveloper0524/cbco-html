CBCO is a Saudi family-owned business established in 1969, delivering trusted solutions in construction, ready-mix concrete, precast manufacturing, and diversified industries, supporting Saudi Arabia's growth and Vision 2030.

About Us

Family Business Started in 1969 by its founder Shaikh Saleh Ben Abdullah Al-Dakheel in Qassim Region in Construction contracting with Aluminum & Carpentering Workshops. After that, the business expanded to Ready Mix Concrete plants & Precast Plant supported the developments in the region.

In 2013 the business restructured into Joint Stock Company managed by the second generation of the Family and diversified into several sectors targeting Saudi Arabia Vision 2030.

CBCO is one of the Family Consortium Companies developed & managed by Second & Third Generation.

We will develop & operate companies that provide its customers with superior quality products & services. As a result of customers reward, we will add value to the communities we are living on.

Cement Trading

01

Cement Trading

CBCO is a distributor for several Cement Plants in Saudi Arabia and supplying its customers in Central, Westen & Eastern regions of Saudi Arabia utilizing its fleets & Bulkers with the following products:

  • Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC) is the most common cement used in general concrete construction when there is no exposure to sulphates in the soil or groundwater.
  • Sulphate Resisting Portland Cement is a type of Portland Cement in which the amount of some active ingredientsis lower than usual
  • The Portland Pozzolan Cement (PPC) is a kind of blended cement which is produced by inter grinding of OPC clinker along with gypsum and pozzolanic materials in certain proportions.
  • Finishing cement is appropriate for various purposes like finishing jobs, plaster, masonry, tile works, and flooring. It is specially used for finishing work.
